Our Team

We’re twin brothers, Emirhan Topçu and Bedirhan Topçu, and we’ve been capturing fashion through our lenses since 2021. Working together, we blend our styles and ideas to create unique, bold images. Whether it’s for a campaign or a lookbook, we’re all about delivering visuals that stand out and make an impact.

Our Mission

As Gunner Brothers, our mission is to showcase the artistry of fashion through the lens of creative vision and technical expertise. We aim to bring out the essence of every design, model, and moment, delivering striking images that captivate and inspire. Whether shooting for editorial spreads, lookbooks, or high-end campaigns, our goal is to elevate fashion into visual storytelling that speaks to both brands and audiences.
